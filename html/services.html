Systems Engineer

WCBinc systems engineering competency is based on sound engineering processes that span the full systems engineering lifecycle. Our strengths include the following: architecture design; network design and implementation; process development; Department of Defense milestone acquisition documentation; configuration management; system integration; independent verification and validation; and requirements development and management. Our engineering processes blend systems engineering best practices with project management best practices based on the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK) from the Project Management Institute. Combining systems engineering and project management into one cohesive end-to-end process set. By instituting the WCBinc methodology WCBinc systems engineers are able to provide our clients with comprehensive solutions. This combined methodology sets WCBinc systems engineers apart from other engineers and project managers.

Research and Development

The patented WCBinc Empowered Solutions Development (ESD) teams assist our customers in brainstorming, identifying, documenting, tracing requirements, developing new standards and provisioning solutions that meet and exceed existing needs and provide room for scalability in the future.

Data Center Infrastructure Management (DCIM)

WCBinc DCIM Teams provide full life cycle DCIM objectives of Energy, Asset, Availability, Risk, Service, Supply Chain and IT Automation Management. WCBinc Engineers manage assets from acquisition to decommissioning, assist in reducing energy costs, proactively identify pending failures, schedule recurring maintenance outages to comply with Service Level Agreements, establish compliance with DOD and IC regulations and monitor Service Requests to assist in implementation of corrective actions to ensure high availability. Additionally, WCBinc Engineers can enhance system automation for the planning and execution of infrastructure service requests, eliminating manual steps and speeding time-to-delivery.
